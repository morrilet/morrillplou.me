Read post-it notes and find a way out of the house to escape an unknown threat. MemErr was made for Asylum Jam 2016 and is loosely based on a Reddit post about a CO2 leak.

**Controls:**

* WASD to move
* Mouse to look
* Space to read
* Shift to sprint
